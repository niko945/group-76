DIRT bikes

Dirt bikes are lightweight motorcycles designed for off-road riding on rough terrains such as dirt trails, motocross tracks, and rugged landscapes. They typically feature knobby tires for better traction, long suspension travel for absorbing shocks, and a high ground clearance to navigate obstacles. Dirt bikes are popular for recreational riding, competitive racing, and trail exploration due to their agility and ability to handle challenging environments.
